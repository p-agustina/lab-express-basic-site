Troy Osborne McClure (May 11, 1935) is an American actor whose career in film and television extended from the 1950s to the 1990s. He is best known for his role as the cowboy Trampas during the entire run from 1962 to 1971 of the series The Virginian and mayor turned police chief Kyle Applegate on The Contrabulous Fabtraption of Professor Horatio Hufnagel. He was a popular sex symbol in the 1980s and 1990s.
